Patna-capital of Bihar

Description-

Patna (English: /ˈpʌtnə, ˈpæt-/,[13][14] Hindi: [ˈpəʈnaː] ⓘ), historically known as Pataliputra,[15] is the capital and largest city of the state of Bihar in India.[1] According to the United Nations, as of 2018, Patna had a population of 2.35 million,[16] making it the 19th largest city in India.[1] Covering 250 square kilometres (97 sq mi) and over 2.5 million people, its urban agglomeration is the 15th largest in India. Patna also serves as the seat of Patna High Court
